BezierPatch

class BezierPatch extends Surface

Defines a Bézier patch of degree (3, 3), that is, one with four control points along each parameter.

    control

    the control points of the patch, arranged in u-major order, i.e. indices 0-3 are all along the top u-edge, etc. Note that the ordering of the vertices will determine the direction of the faces and the normals; the normal direction is roughly -[(P_03 - P_00) cross (P_30 - P_00)].
